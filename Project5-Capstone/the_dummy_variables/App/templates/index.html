What is Orpheus?

Meet Orpheus:

Orpheus is a multi-user music recommendation system based on mood. An implicit model is created using collaborative filtering by collecting the listening history of users and outputting a recommendation based on that listening history. To get a group recommendation, an average aggregation strategy was used on heterogenous groups.
